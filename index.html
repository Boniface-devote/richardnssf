About Us

Nsawo Agri Company exists to support and empower farmers by providing high-quality agricultural machinery and engineering solutions.

Mission

To provide accessible, innovative, and tailored agricultural engineering solutions that empower farmers to achieve sustainable productivity and contribute to food security and economic growth.

Vision

To become a trusted partner in transforming agriculture in Uganda and beyond, leveraging innovation and engineering to create resilient and prosperous farming communities.

Goal

To enhance agricultural productivity and profitability by providing farmers with high-quality machinery, innovative solutions, and technical support that meet their unique needs, contributing to sustainable rural development and food security.

Our Services

Supply of Agricultural Machinery

Tractors, plows, harrows, planters, harvesters, and irrigation systems.

Maintenance and Repair Services

Routine maintenance and repair of agricultural equipment to ensure optimal performance.

Custom Equipment Fabrication

Designing and fabricating equipment tailored to specific farming needs.

Consultation and Advisory

Providing expert advice on selecting, operating, and maintaining machinery.

Training for Farmers

Educating farmers and operators on machinery usage and safety protocols.

Leasing and Rental Services

Offering affordable rental options for machinery to farmers.

Spare Parts Supply

Supplying high-quality and affordable spare parts for various agricultural machines.
